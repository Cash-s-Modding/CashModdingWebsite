Skip Ads Mod

Skips all ads on youtube

When you click the bookmark it will hide all ads and banners on youtube

How to get it

1- Copy all of this script:

javascript: if(document.getElementsByClassName("video-ads")[0].innerHTML !==""){ var banner = false; for(var i = 0; i < document.getElementsByClassName("ytp-ad-overlay-close-button").length; i++){ document.getElementsByClassName("ytp-ad-overlay-close-button")[i].click(); banner = true;} if(banner === false){ document.getElementsByClassName("html5-main-video")[0].currentTime = document.getElementsByClassName("html5-main-video")[0].duration; document.getElementsByClassName("ytp-ad-skip-button")[0].click();} } void 0;


2- In your browser click the 3 dots in the top right.
Then hover over bookmarks and then click bookmark manager.


3- In the bookmark manager click the 3 dots in the top right.
Then click Add New Bookmark.


4- For the title put anything you want. (Im putting Skip Ads)
Then paste the code into the url.
After that click save.


-How to use it-
Click the bookmark in your bookmarks bar. (If you do not see it do Ctrl+Shift+B)
It will then hide all ads and banners even if they are not skippable
