Instant Word V2 Mod

Types in answer when on wordle

When you click the bookmark it will find and type in the answer to the wordle

How to get it

1- Download this file:

InstantWord.html


2- In your browser click the 3 dots in the top right.
Then hover over bookmarks and then click bookmark manager.


3- In the bookmark manager click the 3 dots in the top right.
Then click Import Bookmarks


4- Select the file you just downloaded.


-How to use it-
Click the bookmark in your bookmarks bar. (If you do not see it do Ctrl+Shift+B)
It will type in the word of the day (Only on the wordle website)
